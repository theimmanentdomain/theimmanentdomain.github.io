So the immanent domain is a religious entity.
The act of creation is art making. Art is not a product, art is a process, according to the imminent
domain. And any works made under its banner do so under the premise of fostering the
sublime, the divine, by bringing the transcendent and making it immediate.
Transcendence itself is not a goal. Transcendence is also, much like art, a process. The goal is
the place where you would rather be.
Oftentimes we find that once we open our hearts, many of our desires are false, but there are
true desires of the heart that will never go away. The heart yearns for things that are ineffable,
undefinable. The second we try to name the quelling of the heart is the second we doom
ourselves into the prison of the imagination self. We objectify things. We objectify others.
Idiots worshipping idols, or just a permutation of nature itself, simply fizzling out, as it were, until
eventually it stops, and all is forgotten and lost. No lessons learned. As if the universe won't
remember in one way or another, because it does. Because cause and effect is a brain unto
itself.
That's the whole point of ineffable. The so you understand that it's better to gaze in wonder at
the sublime than it is to try to identify or classify elements of it. That's the reason why voyeurs
will never make it into this little slice of heaven.
The flame tongue constantly kisses you, even if the tip of the flame doesn't go anywhere near
your skin.
