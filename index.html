FUNCTION

01 / MANIFESTO

Street-level doctrine

I

The anti-algorithm

“There is a secret place for everyone.”

“We no longer negotiate with the numinous.”

“We have become product.”

II

The condition

The contemporary cultural field runs on extraction. Algorithms flatten attention into metrics. Venues optimize for sales rather than atmosphere. Art circulates as brand rather than presence.

III

The turn

Technology has made creative resources freely available to everyone. The streets, the internet, anywhere and everywhere is our stage.

IV

The work

We band together so that we can freely and endlessly create and distribute novelty, perspective, and dismantle the cynicism of the postmodern.

V

The method

Sound, light, bodies, language, architecture, and timing converge to create a pocket of heightened reality.

VI

The promise

Performances, publications, installations, and events act as carriers of the deeper function: the transmission of meaning through lived experience.

04 / ABOUT

What this is

The Immanent Domain operates as a creative-intelligence agency that uses art as a medium of communication rather than as a product.

Its output takes the form of performances, publications, objects, situations, and zones where people feel something organize inside them.

This site functions as the public face of that system: manifesto, editorial platform, archive, and signal point.
