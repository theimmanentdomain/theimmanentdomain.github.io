04 / ABOUT

What this is

The Immanent Domain operates as an editorial object and a field report. This version keeps the text forward, the layout severe, and the structure ready for expansion.

The site is intentionally static. No blog engine. No template gloss. Just HTML, CSS, and the uploaded texts arranged into a public front.

That makes it easy to edit on GitHub, easy to expand, and hard to bloat.
